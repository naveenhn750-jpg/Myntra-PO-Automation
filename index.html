Myntra PO Workflow

Upload master + PO files, then download the final mapped workbook.

This tool combines all uploaded PO files, adds the PO-wise Ship To location, maps values from the master file, checks HSN, EAN, and NLC with GST, ignores NLC differences within -1 to +1, and generates a downloadable Excel file for your team.
